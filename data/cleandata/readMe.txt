CLEAN DATASETS INFO

* patient_PRS: available genetic data (Polygenic Risk Scores) of ADNI participants.
* patientCharacteristics: clean demographic data of ADNI participants, merged with genetic PRS data.
* volumesAndPRS: hippocampal subfield volume measurements of ADNI participants, merged with genetic and demographic data.
* dataForAnalysis: a subset of volumeAndPRS datasets to be used in analysis (some data management and minor cleaning tasks are performed) in 3T data.
* dataForAnalysis_1T: a subset of volumeAndPRS datasets to be used in analysis (some data management and minor cleaning tasks are performed) in 1.5T data.
* ADNIPathsInfo: it contains in which phase a certain participant enroll in ADNI, and for how long (follow up phases).
